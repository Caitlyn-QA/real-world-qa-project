# Sprint 1 Review

## Sprint

**Sprint 1 – Cart Reliability**

## Sprint Goal

Ensure that customers can add products to their cart and manage cart contents without losing items or seeing incorrect product, quantity or price information.

## Sprint Goal Status

**Partially achieved**

Sprint 1 validated the core add-to-cart behaviour for one product and confirmed cart persistence after refresh within the same browser session.

However, the full cart-management scope was not completed. Additional scenarios are required before the cart flow can be considered fully validated.

## Completed Work

Sprint 1 included:

- Business requirement review
- User story and acceptance criteria review
- Test analysis
- Exploratory testing
- Initial Playwright automation
- Cart validation for one product
- Cart persistence check after refresh
- Sprint 1 reflection

## Not Completed in Sprint 1

The following areas were not fully validated during Sprint 1:

- Adding multiple products
- Removing products from the cart
- Updating product quantities
- Broader cart-management behaviour
- Smoke test coverage
- Regression test coverage
- Bug discovery and defect reporting

## Review Summary

Sprint 1 created a solid foundation for the QA project and validated the most critical add-to-cart path.

The sprint also showed that the original sprint goal was broader than the completed scope.

The remaining cart scenarios and missing QA activities should be reviewed during Sprint 2 planning.

## Next Steps

Sprint 2 should focus on expanding the QA workflow and improving feature coverage, including:

- More complete cart testing
- Smoke testing
- Regression testing
- Bug reporting
- Clearer decisions about automation scope and timing
